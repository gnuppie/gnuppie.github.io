Hello there!

My name is Louis and I'm a freshmore student based in Singapore, pursuing my Bachelor of Engineering in the Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), majoring in Information Systems,Technology and Design.

I love all things food-related, whether it be cooking, consuming, searching for new foodie spots or watching videos on YouTube. I am particularly crazy about cheese, curry and beef.
